QR Code Generator & Reader

This app is your all-in-one solution for generating, customizing, scanning, and managing QR codes. Whether you're sharing contact details, connecting to Wi-Fi, or creating dynamic codes for your business — this app makes it fast and easy.

Designed with a modern interface and powered by the latest Android technologies, it supports a wide variety of QR formats and includes customization tools for a personalized look.

Key Features

  • Generate QR codes for text, URLs, contacts, emails, Wi-Fi, and more
  • Scan and decode QR codes instantly with camera or image import
  • Customize colors, patterns, and add logos
  • Batch QR generation for businesses or marketing
  • History tracking and favorite QR codes
  • Export and share in high-quality formats
  • Built with Jetpack Compose and modern Android architecture
