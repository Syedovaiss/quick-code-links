QR Code Generator & Reader

Welcome! This app is a smart and simple tool designed to help you create, customize, and scan QR codes with ease. Whether you're an individual sharing contact details or a business promoting products, this app offers everything you need in one place.

From connecting to Wi-Fi in a tap, to sharing social profiles, or even creating professional-looking QR codes with your brand’s logo — we’ve got you covered.

What You Can Do

  • Create QR codes for links, emails, texts, locations, and more
  • Scan codes using your camera or from saved images
  • Customize QR colors, shapes, and even add your own logo
  • Save your generated codes in history for quick access
  • Generate multiple QR codes at once (batch feature)
  • Export and share QR codes easily with friends or customers

Why Use This App?

  • Easy to Use – Designed for everyone, no tech skills needed
  • Privacy First – Your data stays on your device
  • Flexible – Supports a wide range of QR types
  • Stylish – Add a personal touch with colors and logos
  • Fast & Reliable – Scan and generate in seconds

Whether you're organizing an event, launching a business, or just want to have fun with QR codes — this app is built to help you do it better and faster.
